label: Why Day.js?
---

- **Lightweight**: ~2KB in size, making it one of the smallest date libraries.
- **Immutable**: Operations do not modify the original date, ensuring data integrity.
- **Chainable API**: Supports chaining multiple methods for clean, readable code.
- **I18n Support**: Provides localization support for various languages.
- **Plugin System**: Extendable with plugins for additional functionality, like relative time or duration.
- **Modern**: Works with both browser and Node.js environments.
