In this article, you will learn how to integrate Superflow with Slack.

Access the Superflow Dashboard by going to [app.usesuperflow.com](app.usesuperflow.com). Alternatively, when you are in Review Mode, you can use the method below:

1. Find the **Superflow Toolbar** and click the **More Options** icon.

 
![](https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1733131835055/7wB0vNvxr.png?auto=format)
 

2. A sub-menu will expand, click the **View Project** option to select.

3. A new tab will direct you to the **Project Dashboard** page.

> **Note**: By default, you will be directed to the Dashboard for Comment Status screen when accessing the **Project Dashboard**.

4. Click the **INTEGRATIONS** module to access more integration options.

 
![](https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1733137081062/lkvVe2Pom.png?auto=format)
 

5. Select **SLACK** to access Slack’s integration page and click **Connect** to start the integration.

6. You will be directed to Slack’s integration page. In the **Workspace** field, enter the workspace’s URL and click **Continue**.

 
![](https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1733137099586/aZSdqFGqA.png?auto=format)
 

7. You will be directed to the confirmation page, click **Allow** to allow Superflow to access the specified workspace.

 
![](https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1733137116284/ORMYaRivI.png?auto=format)
