## How to Integrate with ClickUp

In this article, you will learn how to integrate Superflow with ClickUp.

Access the Superflow Dashboard by going to [app.usesuperflow.com](app.usesuperflow.com). Alternatively, when you are in Review Mode, you can use the method below:

1. Find the **Superflow Toolbar** and click the **More Options** icon.

<Frame>
![](https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1733131835055/7wB0vNvxr.png?auto=format)
</Frame>

2. A sub-menu will expand, click the **View Project** option to select.

3. A new tab will direct you to the **Project Dashboard** page.

> **Note**: By default, you will be directed to the Dashboard for Comment Status screen when accessing the **Project Dashboard**.

4. Click the **INTEGRATIONS** module to access more integration options.

<Frame>
![](https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1733136887927/p2DHKO0l9.png?auto=format)
</Frame>

5. Select **CLICKUP** to access ClickUpâ€™s integration page and click **Connect** to start the integration.

6. For this example, we will use a Gmail account. Click the **Sign in with Google** button.

> **Note**: If you are using non-Google email, use the **Email** and **Password** fields to continue

<Frame>
![](https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1733136907531/X3CHdmugu.png?auto=format)
</Frame>

7. You will be directed to the **Sign in with Google** page, find and select the email that you want to use.

<Frame>
![](https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1733136922961/sTUxy_gcU.png?auto=format)
</Frame>

8. You will be directed to the **Confirmation** page, click **Confirm** to allow Superflow to access your ClickUp account.

<Frame>
![](https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1733136941336/Aw92ktVJV.png?auto=format)
</Frame>

9. You will be directed to the **Connect ClickUp** page, click the **Connect Workspace** button to complete the integration.

<Frame>
![](https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1733136955609/yMwIZ5NJE.png?auto=format)
</Frame>
