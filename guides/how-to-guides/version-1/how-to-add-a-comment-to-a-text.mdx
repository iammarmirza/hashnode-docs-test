## How to Add a Comment to a Text

In this article, you will learn how to add comments to a text.

1. Highlight the **Text** you want to comment on using your cursor. After highlighting the text, click the **Comment** icon on the enabled **Toolbar**.

 
![](https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1733291851628/W1bWXUGUF.png?auto=format)
 

2. The **Comment Box** will appear, enter your comment or type **@** to add other users.

> **Note**: the **Comment Box** lets you add users, attach files, and record audio, videos, and screens.

 
![](https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1733291897811/PsluF431P.png?auto=format)
 

3. Click the **Post** button to post your comment.
