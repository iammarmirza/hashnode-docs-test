## How to Switch to Mobile View

In this article, you will learn how to switch to mobile view. This option enables you to access a page you are working on from your mobile device by accessing the generated QR Code.

1. Find the **Superflow Toolbar** and click the **More Options** icon.

 
![](https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1733293425082/jcr25IpqM.png?auto=format)
 

2. A sub-menu will expand, click the **Open in Mobile** to select.

3. A **QR Code** will appear on the screen. On your mobile device, open the **Camera** app and scan the **QR Code**.

 
![](https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1733293443532/cqSE6-9D8.png?auto=format)
 

4. A **Link** will appear, select the link and the mobile version of the current screen of your desktop/laptop will appear on your mobile device.

 
![](https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1733293471465/EMV2lGqE2.png?auto=format)
