## How to Record Voice Notes

In this article, you will learn how to record voice notes using the Superflow Toolbar.

1. Find the **Superflow Toolbar** and click the **Comment** icon.

 
![](https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1733293099617/hrrqOyilj.png?auto=format)
 

2. After clicking the icon, click the **Element** you want to comment on.

> **Note**: when you hover your cursor on an element, it will appear as a **Comment** icon which signifies that you selected the comment feature.

 
![](https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1733293115334/JDql5ipP8.png?auto=format)
 

3. The **Comment Box** will appear, click the **Microphone** icon to select.

 
![](https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1733293131245/wmnkiBLkd.png?auto=format)
 

4. A **Recording** window will appear. Click the **Start Recording** button to begin recording the voice note.

 
![](https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1733293148809/2ngXGXoL2.png?auto=format)
 

5. To complete your recording, click the **Green Check Mark**. This will stop the current recording.

 
![](https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1733293174615/P33guEf4I.png?auto=format)
 

6. Click the **Post** button to save the voice note.

 
![](https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1733293188617/AIB10Mtxw.png?auto=format)
